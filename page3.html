Properties

Sodium has a silver luster, and it is smooth. It is a solid at room temperature. The melting temperature of sodium is 97.72 degrees Celsius. The boiling temperature is 883 degrees Celsius! Wow!

Sodium is found in the compounds NaCl, table salt, NaOh, caustic soda, and also NaHCO3, which is baking soda. It is also used for glass, soap, paper, and to purify molten metals. Sodium is obtained by being chemically separated from a compound.
